About Us

The founder of Pug's Adoption, Mr. Perez, has always had a passion for pugs. He always wanted to a pug but did not have the opportunity to own one until he was older. Mr. Perez decided to make it so anyone could have an easy way of adopting a pug. We choose all of our staff carefully to make sure your pug gets all the best attention it deserves.
